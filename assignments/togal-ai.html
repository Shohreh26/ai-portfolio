Summary

This assignment used Togal.AI for quantity takeoffs on five assigned drawings. Each submission PDF combines AI-generated takeoffs with one or two manual corrections to the largest errors, clearly marked so a colleague can follow what changed. Takeoffs were developed from the tool’s outputs and graphs generated in the workflow.

Assignment (course requirements)

First, upload your Togal.AI quantity takeoffs for each of the five drawings that were assigned.

Each deliverable should be a single PDF that shows the AI-performed quantity takeoffs plus one or two corrections you made manually to the biggest issues you noticed in the AI takeoffs. Clearly mark or label those corrections so they are easy for a colleague to view and understand.

Workflow notes

Graphs generated in Togal.AI were used to support quantity takeoff review and adjustment. The PDFs below are the packaged exports for each drawing type and building program.
